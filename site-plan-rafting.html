Overview

Purpose

This website aims to highlight the beauty of Idaho's rivers and nature while promoting the excitement of white water rafting. It encourages visitors to experience nature's grandeur and raises awareness of environmental conservation through sustainable outdoor activities.

Audience

This website is aimed at a broad audience, ranging from families with children to college students and beyond. It caters to those who seek outdoor adventure while appreciating the beauty of Idaho's natural landscapes. Whether they are beginners or seasoned enthusiasts, the site offers valuable information for anyone interested in white water rafting and learning about environmental conservation.
